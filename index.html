About Me

RSS Sangathan Pad Adhikari & Social Worker

I am Mahesh Kathore, a dedicated social worker and RSS (Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh) leader from Khandwa district, Madhya Pradesh. For over 15 years, I have been actively working as an RSS Sangathan Pad Adhikari, contributing to social service, cultural awareness, and organizational development.

My journey in RSS began during my college days, inspired by the organization's commitment to nation-building and cultural preservation. Over the years, I have taken on various responsibilities, working closely with rural communities, organizing youth development programs, and participating in disaster relief efforts.

2005

Joined RSS as Swayamsevak

Began my journey with RSS during college days

2010

Appointed as Nagar Karyavah

Promoted to Sangathan Pad Adhikari

Vision & Mission

My vision is to contribute to building a strong, self-reliant India that stands tall on the foundation of its ancient cultural values while embracing technological progress and social harmony.

I believe in the RSS philosophy of "वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम" (the world is one family) and strive to work towards a society where every individual has the opportunity to grow while maintaining our cultural identity.

Social Service

To serve the society selflessly, especially the underprivileged sections, through various initiatives and programs.

Organization Building

To strengthen the organizational network of RSS and its affiliates for nation-building activities.

Cultural Awareness

To promote awareness about Indian culture, traditions, and values among the youth.

Message to the Youth

Dear youth of India, you are the future of our nation. I urge you to connect with our cultural roots while embracing modern education and technology. Participate in nation-building activities, develop a spirit of service, and work towards making India a Vishwa Guru again. Remember, development without cultural grounding is like a tree without roots.

Join organizations like RSS that provide the right platform for character building and national service.
